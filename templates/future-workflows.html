Future Workflows

Circulation Migration: Loans and Requests

Using the folio_migration_tools support for migrating loans and requests, we plan on adding DAGs to support our own migration of loans and requests from Symphony to FOLIO.

Migrating Bills

Patron bills for such fines, missing, or damaged materials will be imported into FOLIO through one or more DAGs in Airflow.

Ongoing MARC Record and User Loads

By using the scheduling capabilities and the extensible nature of Airflow, we hope that we build DAGs for migrating MARC bibliographic records that may require customization beyond what is available in mod-import.

Likewise, our current method of importing users from Stanford's registrar office is a manual process and so we want to leverage our existing tools to automate these user loads into FOLIO.

Authority Records

Just as we have automated MARC bibliographic record loading int FOLIO with Airflow, we plan to have a similar functionality for importing MARC authority records into FOLIO.

Further Sinopia Integration

Our current integration between Sinopia and FOLIO is very basic with only BIBFRAME Instances and Works resources being exported out of Sinopia and into FOLIO. Future plans include:

  • Create richer FOLIO Instance records from available BIBFRAME RDF
  • Generate appropriate FOLIO Holdings records.
  • Explore generating FOLIO Item records from BIBFRAME Item resources for such properties as Barcode and other item specific identifiers.
