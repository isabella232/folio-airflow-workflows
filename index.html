Stanford University Libraries is currently migrating from legacy ILS to FOLIO as well as developing methods for integrating the Sinopia Linked Data Editor with FOLIO. To meet these diverse requirements, we have adopted the open-source workflow automation tool Airflow. Airflow allows us to automate the harvesting and extracting of MARC21 and RDF data from these external systems to FOLIO Inventory Instances, Holdings, and Items records by writing Python-based directed acyclic graphs (DAG).
